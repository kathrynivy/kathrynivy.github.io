Knitting Journal

April 14, 2008 - Posted by Alice Schnebly

Kathryn Ivy is 1 year old!

We can't believe it was one year ago today that we started this blog! It has turned out to be much more fun and fulfilling than we ever expected it to be. The people we've met along the way have been awesome, and we can't wait to make our second year even better!

We'd like to thank you guys for coming by so we've put together a little giveaway for our readers both new and old. Feel free to enter even if you've never commented or just started reading us yesterday—the more the merrier! Everyone from all parts of the world can enter as well.

We've each put together a prize package so there will be two winners! First name drawn will get to choose which one they'd like.

Grace's package includes:
1 skein Fiesta La Luz (100% silk) in color #17105 Amazon
10 skeins Berroco Soft Twist (41% wool, 59% rayon) in color #9415 Pilaster
1 skein Cascade 220 Superwash (100% superwash wool) in color #875 Stone
Choice of Hawkeye or Kiddie Cadet Hat Pattern

Alice’s package includes:
2 skeins hand-dyed Cascade 220 (100% wool)
2 skeins Jo-Ann Sensations Cello (100% polyester) in Black
1 skein Patons Classic Merino Wool (100% wool) in Black
1 skein Patons Classic Merino Wool (100% wool) in Too Teal
1 package 21mm Animal Noses
1 package 12mm Animal Eyes
1 skein Cascade 220 Superwash (100% superwash wool) in color #869
Choice of Hawkeye or Kiddie Cadet Hat Pattern
1 Little Lamb Finger Puppet by Soto Softies (not pictured)

All you have to do is leave a comment sharing what pattern you've had in your queue or on your "to-knit" list the longest. Comments will be accepted until Monday April 21 at Noon PST, when we'll draw the winners!

We'd also like to recognize some people who really supported us and kept us going through our first year. Thanks to Kari for profiling us in her Blogger Spotlight, Adrienne for her super sweet interview, Craftster for featuring Alice's Easter Egg Dyed Yarn in the Best of 2007, Craftzine for featuring Grace's Harry Bear, DPN Holders, and Mushrooms, all of our friends on Flickr and Ravelry, and everyone who's stopped by and commented on our blog! Thanks for making it a great year!
